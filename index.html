Nephele is a belly dancer with a passion for teaching.
Join her community classes in Ealing Community Hall - open to beginners of all ages, levels of ability, size, and gender.

Nephele started learning belly dance at the age of 16 in her native Greece. Since then she has continued to learn and perform a variety of styles. With a strong foundation in Egyptian cabaret and Greek chifteteli, Nephele has also explored a variety of props like fanveils and swords. She is specifically interested in improvisation, as she believes this is the best way to connect with the roots of this ancient dance form. She is currently studying Tribal Fusion, in preparation of Jill Parker's Alchemy intensive cerification programme in August 2019 in Brighton.

Nephele's classes are great for beginners. We focus on safe practice whilst having fun.

Q: What can I expect from the class?
A: Expect an upbeat, supportive, encouraging environment. You will break a sweat whilst having fun,
improve your body awareness, coordination, and learn a new skill.

Q: I've never danced before - can I still come?
A: Absolutely! Everyone is welcome. Nephele cultivates an environment of body positivity and inclusivity in her classes!

Q: What should I wear?
A: Comfortable clothes you can freely move in. Most students wear leggings and a top. Please bring a scarf to tie around your hips - or if you're up for it, a coinbelt!
